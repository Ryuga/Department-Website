About Us

The Computer Science Department of Christ College offers B.Sc. Computer Science, BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application), M.Sc. Computer Science , Certificate Courses (in Android, Excel 2019 and Data Analytics) and Foundation Course in Computer Fundamentals and ICT Training. B.Sc. Computer Science has started in the year 2012. BCA has started in the year 2013. M.Sc. has started in the year 2017.BVOC IT has started in the year 2018.Besides above mentioned courses Computer Science is also taught as complementary subject for B.Sc. Mathematics and B.Sc. Physics.

Our Vision
  • To impart high quality professional training in undergraduate level with emphasis on basic principles of Computer Science and Applications.

  • To inculcate professional behavior, strong ethical values and leadership abilities in the young minds so as to work with a commitment to the progress of the nation.
Our Mission
  • Imparting quality education in the field of Computer Science and Applications.

  • To impart to students the logical and analytical skills required to have a competitive advantage in the field of Information Technology.

  • To mould students to become ethically responsible individuals and use technology sensibly.

  • To educate students to adapt to the changing dynamics of IT industry.

  • To encourage students to use knowledge gained to contribute to society.
